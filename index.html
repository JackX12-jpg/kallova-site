What we do

Kallova is a software company focused on home services. Our first product is a lawn-care marketplace that measures a yard from its address alone, prices the job upfront, and connects homeowners with local landscaping professionals.

No site visits to get a quote, no phone tag, no guesswork — for homeowners or for the pros doing the work. We're launching first in Massachusetts, with more regions to follow.

Instant yard measurement

Enter an address and we measure the lawn using aerial imagery and public parcel data — accurate sizing before anyone sets foot on the property.

Upfront, fair pricing

Transparent per-visit pricing based on the actual yard, shown before you book. No estimates, no surprises on the invoice.

Local professionals

Jobs go to vetted landscaping pros nearby, who see the work clearly scoped and get paid promptly when it's done.
